Our Story

Why we practice this way

Most of our patients arrive after years of being told their labs are "normal" while their lives are anything but. They are tired, foggy, in pain, or simply not themselves — and they want someone to actually look.

Functional medicine is built around that kind of looking. We run thorough lab work, ask the questions a rushed appointment can't, and treat the body as a system rather than a checklist. The goal is never just to silence a symptom. It is to understand what your body is trying to tell you, and to help it work the way it was designed to.

How It Works

A clear, unhurried path forward

A complimentary 15-minute conversation. We learn what you're working with, you learn how we practice, and we decide together if it's a fit. No pressure, no pitch.

A full hour with Emily. We go through your history, your symptoms, your goals — and we order targeted lab work to fill in the picture. Most patients leave this visit feeling heard for the first time.

We sit down with your results and walk through what they mean. You leave with a written, prioritized plan — supplements, lifestyle protocols, and any further testing — designed to fit your life.

Real change takes time. We work alongside you — through coaching visits, follow-up labs, and adjustments to your plan — until you're not just better, but well.
