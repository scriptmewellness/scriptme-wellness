Our Approach

We listen first.
Then we test, never guess.

Conventional care moves fast. Functional medicine moves carefully. We start with a long, unhurried conversation — your history, your symptoms, your goals — and use targeted lab work to understand what's actually happening in your body.

From there, we build a plan that fits your life. Realistic, sustainable, and grounded in evidence. You'll always know what we're doing and why.

What We Help With

Conditions we focus on

Hormonal imbalance shows up in dozens of ways — fatigue, cycle irregularities, mood, weight, sleep. We use comprehensive testing to map what's happening and rebuild balance from the inside out.

Standard thyroid panels miss most of the picture. We run full labs — TSH, free T3, free T4, antibodies, reverse T3 — and address the upstream drivers: nutrient status, gut health, stress, and inflammation.

Bloating, irregularity, food sensitivities, and chronic inflammation often trace back to the gut. We use targeted testing to identify imbalances and rebuild the microbiome from the ground up.

Sustainable change starts upstream. We address insulin sensitivity, hormones, sleep, and stress — not just calories — so the results last.

Functional medicine works alongside conventional care for autoimmune disease, chronic fatigue, fibromyalgia, and other complex conditions where root-cause work makes a real difference.
